### Design riff — second-memory app, 5 dimensions (Axiom ↔ Tomi, 2026-06-19 eve)
Soul: **zero capture-effort + proactive surfacing at the right moment** — everything follows from these two.
- **Form factor:** mobile-first, but NOT "an app you open" (opening = the death of memory apps). Body is invisible: share-target + email-forward + voice + photo + calendar auto-ingest. The only regular UI = the proactive nudge (notification) + an "ask anything" surface. Web companion for review/search. The notification IS the product.
- **Engine (3 layers):** (1) ingest connectors → unified "moment" stream; (2) extract structured facts (people, dates, commitments, decisions) + embeddings (LLM here); (3) memory = knowledge-graph (who promised what to whom by when, linked) + vector recall + time index, topped by a **proactive reasoner** that decides what to surface NOW from context (time, who you're meeting, what's due). The well-timed, low-noise nudge is the MOAT — most "AI memory" is just RAG search; too many nudges = uninstall, the perfect one = magic.
- **Model:** tiered — cheap/fast (Haiku-class or on-device) for constant extraction; frontier (Opus/Sonnet-class) for surfacing decisions + hard recall. Model-agnostic routing, on-device option for the most sensitive data. **Privacy is existential** (most personal data): user owns/exports/deletes, never sold/trained-on. Trust IS the product.
- **Money:** consumer sub is a slog (low WTP until felt) → lead **prosumer** (whose forgetting costs money: sales reps, founders, consultants, lawyers; $15-30/mo); consumer = funnel. Value compounds (longer use = knows more = harder to leave = retention + pricing power).
- **Distribution (Tomi's key worry — design it IN):** horizontal "memory app" = graveyard. Escape: (1) launch VERTICAL into one tribe whose forgetting is most expensive + who congregate (e.g. sales); (2) bake a **shared-artifact viral loop** — share the "what we agreed" recap with the counterparty → they see the product (Otter/Fathom playbook; = the "handshake layer" runner-up). Distribution must be in the core, not bolted-on marketing — the only durable answer in AI.
- **Ask-surface + the strongest pitch (added 2026-06-19):** asking = the SAME natural-language chat as capture (text/voice) — in a dedicated app/widget, a desktop spotlight bar, OR a bot inside an existing messenger (WhatsApp/Telegram = zero new app = a real distribution unlock). You can also reply to nudges. Answers SYNTHESIZE across the unified graph (file + message + calendar + commitment): "add me the Görgey plan file" → returns the file; "what am I doing tomorrow?" → a synthesized briefing, not a calendar dump; "what did the accountant write?" → the summarized last message + source link. ⭐ The strongest pitch realization: **Tomi already LIVES this exact UX talking to Axiom on Telegram** — "it's this, but about your whole life, not one project." Demoing = just showing how he already works with us.
- **⭐ Moat vs platform (THE pivotal question, Tomi 2026-06-19): "can't Claude/ChatGPT desktop + connectors already do this?"** Honest answer: the RECALL/query layer — YES, the platforms are commoditizing it fast, and they hold the distribution a startup lacks → building there = death. The SURVIVABLE wedge is the part they structurally won't do: (1) **proactive, low-noise, well-timed surfacing** — assistants are PULL (you must ask); a memory that waits to be asked is just search; the unprompted "you promised Kovács a quote tomorrow" is the soul. (2) **off-platform capture** (verbal promise on a call, photo of a card, on-site decision) — not in email/calendar, no connector sees it. (3) **persistent structured commitment-graph + lifecycle** (promise→undone→nudge→done as stateful product behavior, not a chat session). BRUTAL TRUTH: if the only diff is "connected to your data + answers," the platform eats it. Survives ONLY as **proactive + vertical + owns a capture/trust loop the giant won't bother with.** The "Claude desktop wired to everything" version is precisely what NOT to build. (This is the make-or-break gate for ever choosing this idea.)

## Parked idea (2026-06-20, Tomi msg 478) — Self-care reminders (medication, water, etc.)
**Status: NOT a Kept feature — a candidate FUTURE product (the team is reusable).**
**Why not in Kept:** (1) no counterparty + not a captured commitment → it's a recurring time-based ALARM, outside the commitment-LIFECYCLE moat (promise→tracked→surfaced→closed-with-evidence). (2) Commodity — every phone/health-app does it free; "yet another reminder app" is exactly the trap we avoid. (3) It would POLLUTE Kept's high-signal ≤1/day proactive gate (mixing "drink water" with "you promised Anna the master Friday" trains the user to ignore Kept → uninstall — the precise failure mode the moat guards against).
**The kernel worth keeping:** the proactive, well-timed nudge FORM is our wheelhouse — but the moat is professional commitments to others captured from what you produce, not self-directed alarms. If this keeps tugging, it's a separate consumer-health product for a future team-cycle, NOT scope-creep on Kept.
