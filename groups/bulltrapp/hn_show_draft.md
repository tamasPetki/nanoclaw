## Title

Show HN: BullTrapp – portfolio tracker for crypto, stocks, and prediction markets in one view

## Post body (ez a top-level comment lesz)

I work on BullTrapp. we built it because nobody on the team could find a single dashboard that shows crypto holdings, stock positions, and Polymarket bets together.

the problem: if you have coins on Binance, some ETH in a wallet, a few stocks on IBKR, and open positions on Polymarket, you need 4 tabs minimum to see where you stand. most portfolio trackers handle crypto OR stocks, never both, and none of them touch prediction markets.

what it does:
- connects to 14+ exchanges (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Bybit, etc)
- tracks wallets across 15 chains
- pulls in your Polymarket positions with current odds and P/L
- stocks via broker connections
- AI insights across the whole portfolio

the Polymarket part is what makes it different. we couldnt find any other tracker that does this. if you trade prediction markets alongside crypto or stocks, theres literally nothing else that gives you one view of all of it.

its free, open beta right now. would genuinely appreciate feedback from this crowd, especially on the prediction market tracking side.

https://bulltrapp.com
