## FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**BullTrapp Open Beta is Live. Come Build the Future of Portfolio Tracking With Us.**

*Crypto, stocks, prediction markets, AI insights. One dashboard. And we're just getting started.*

**April 2026** — If you trade crypto, hold stocks, bet on Polymarket, or just try to keep track of all of it at once, you know the problem. Your portfolio lives in 5 different tabs. No single tool gives you the full picture.

We built BullTrapp to fix that. And now we're inviting everyone to help us make it better.

**What's already working:**

BullTrapp connects to 14+ crypto exchanges (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, you name it), tracks wallets across 15 blockchains, includes stock portfolio tracking, and does something no other tracker does: it pulls in your Polymarket prediction market positions with real P&L calculations. All of it in one dashboard. Free.

**Where we're going:**

The foundation is ready, but the vision is bigger. We're building BullTrapp into an AI-powered portfolio intelligence tool. Not just tracking what you own, but helping you understand it. Smart alerts, pattern recognition, cross-asset analysis, portfolio optimization suggestions. The kind of stuff that used to be reserved for institutional desks.

And we're not building it alone. The people who join now will shape what this becomes.

**This is an open invitation:**

We're looking for people who want to be part of something early. Not just users. Friends, collaborators, people who care about building good tools.

- If you're a crypto trader who's tired of switching between 5 apps, come tell us what's broken.
- If you trade stocks and crypto and wish one tool could handle both, we want your input.
- If you're into prediction markets and nobody else tracks your Polymarket P&L, we already do.
- If you're a developer or AI enthusiast who wants to help build the next generation of portfolio tools, we'd love to have you.
- If you run a community, a newsletter, a YouTube channel, a Discord server, or a Telegram group, let's partner up. We're genuinely excited to work with anyone who's open to it.

Drop into our Discord, say hi, and let's figure out together what this thing should become. We welcome crypto people, stock traders, prediction market degens, AI nerds, developers, community leaders, and honestly anyone who thinks portfolio tracking could be way better than it is right now.
