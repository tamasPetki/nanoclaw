We had a problem on our team. Half of us trade crypto on multiple exchanges. Some have stocks in a brokerage. A few started playing around with Polymarket prediction markets. And everyone was switching between 4-5 different apps just to get a picture of what they actually own.

So we built BullTrapp.

Its a portfolio control center that pulls everything into one view:

- 14+ crypto exchange integrations (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, etc.)
- 15 blockchain wallet tracking (ETH, SOL, BTC, and more)
- Stock portfolio tracking
- Polymarket prediction market positions with real P&L

That last one is what makes us different. Nobody else tracks prediction market positions alongside your crypto and stocks. If you have money on Polymarket, you know the pain of mentally adding those positions to your "real" portfolio. We just do it automatically.

## The Polymarket angle

Prediction markets are growing fast. Polymarket alone did billions in volume last year. But the tooling around it is still early. You can see your positions on Polymarket itself, but good luck getting a unified view of your net worth when your money is split across Binance, Robinhood, a Ledger wallet, and three Polymarket markets.

We pull all of that into one dashboard with real P&L calculations. You can see exactly how much you're up or down across everything, not just one platform.

## Whats next

We're building this into an AI-powered portfolio intelligence tool. Smart alerts when your portfolio balance shifts, cross-asset pattern recognition, and stuff that used to be institutional-only. The people in our community right now are shaping what gets built.
